About me

My name is Oliva Whittaker. I'm a self taught
Web designer, Web developer, Graphic designer
and Illustrator based in a small town
called Harpenden in The United Kingdom.

In 2014 I graduated from The London College of
Communication with a Bachelors degree in Design
for Graphic Communication,In the same year my
print design for the Cath Kidston YCN Student
Awards Bumble Bee was commended and featured
in the Cath Kidston Summer Exhibition at the
Cath Kidston 180 Piccadilly flagship store.

After this, I studied (Japanese), lived and worked
in Japan as an English Teacher for two and a half years.

In 2017, I returned to my hometown Harpenden
in The United Kingdom and worked freelance
as a Web designer,Web developer,
Graphic designer and Illustrator.

My Hobbies and Interests

In my free time, I like to sing. I usually sing jazz,
vintage and songs from musicals.

I like to illustrate, paint and sketch(animals and still life).

I am learning Japanese and can speak it conversationally.
I enjoy travelling. My favourite place to travel is Japan.

When I have time I also enjoy writing on my blog.

I love all animals and I am interested in animal rights issues.

I enjoy cooking and recently became a vegan.
